Achievements

Super Mario Galaxy 100%


For me, this game is truly the definition of 'nostalgia.' It's a game that has aged well and I hope it will carry the childhood of many more people.

The 100% was fun and not too complicated, I enjoyed it.

Super Mario Galaxy 2 100%


The continuation of 1. was also really good and more complete, even though some levels were complicated.

Getting the 100% was one of the most difficult challenges for me, especially the ultimate last level with 1hp..

Super Mario Odyssey 100%


This game was really perfect from start to the end, I'm happy to have played it at its release on my Switch, it was really fun and varied.

Getting the 100% was really fun and complicated, especially the ultimate level on the moon, but it remained very fun..

Marvel's Spider-Man Remastered


I played this game for the first time in September 2025. I was never really interested in Spider-Man, but this game changed my perspective. Thanks to it, I watched all the Spider-Man movies, including the ones with Miles. I watched everything and really enjoyed it all. I'm happy I started this game!

The 100% was rather long, but really fun; I really enjoyed doing everything in this game.

Marvel's Spider-Man: Miles Morales


After doing the first one, we had to do the next! This game was really good, I noticed a strong improvement in the flying movements, it was smoother and so much fun, even the graphics improved. Even though it was short, it was intense and perfect!

The 100% completion was short, I finished the whole game in a few days, it was super fun but short

Marvel's Spider-Man 2


This game was simply magnificent, I have nothing else to say. Really, everything has been improved, whether it's the characters, the graphics, the movements, honestly the game was incredible.
There are a lot of features that have been added and it really improved the game!

I thought getting 100% would take forever, but I actually managed it in ONE week (playing for several hours a day haha). Honestly, the side missions are so fun and interesting, I really enjoyed doing everything.
